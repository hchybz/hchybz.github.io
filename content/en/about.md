# About ChybzArt

Welcome to my portfolio website! I'm passionate about creation and craftsmanship, combining technical skills with artistic creativity.

## My Background

My experience in IT and datacenters has given me a unique approach to creation: transforming technological equipment into unique furniture pieces and art.

## My Philosophy

I strongly believe in **upcycling** and **revalorization** of materials that would otherwise be discarded. Each server rack, each computer component tells a story, and I strive to give them a second life.

## My Creations

On this site, you'll find two main types of projects:

### 🖥️ IT
Projects related to the world of IT and datacenters: laser-cut logos from recycled equipment, furniture designed from server racks and components.

### 🔨 Non IT
Handcrafted creations in wood and metal: custom furniture, decorative objects, woodworking projects.

### 🎨 Projects
Special projects and unique creations that are out of the ordinary.

## Techniques Used

- **Laser cutting** for precision logos and complex shapes
- **Welding** for metal structure assembly
- **Woodworking** for working with wood (oak, plywood, etc.)
- **Powder coating** for metal finishing
- **3D design** for planning and visualization
