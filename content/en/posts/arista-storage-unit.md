Before ending up in a classic "metals" dumpster, these blanking panels to ensure ventilation of ARISTA chassis and these proprietary Hitachi rails (which only mount on Hitachi racks) found a second-first life in my workshop.

After transforming the panels to make them "closed" and a bit of methodology to create a series of 64 compartments... a storage unit. Warning about weight... wall mounting required.

100% metal, 100% very heavy!
