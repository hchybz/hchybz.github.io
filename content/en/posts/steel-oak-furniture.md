Let's change gears once again: furniture.

A series of furniture pieces made from steel and laminated oak.

Inspired by a collection from a well-known northern French retailer's catalog from years ago 🤣.

## Quality Materials

Except I made these with real materials:
- 2mm tube in 40x40 and 45x45 for benches and table
- 40x20 tube for the coffee table
- Laminated oak tops in 22mm and 44mm for table and benches
- Removable extension integrated under the table
- Edges trimmed with router (my new best friend!)

## Built to Last

Very heavy construction that will last. The "client" seems delighted 😁
