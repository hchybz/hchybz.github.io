Small scraps, of unequal lengths, from a previous project... No waste. On the occasion of acquiring a new metal band saw, a small precision exercise to make this infinite cube. Many sites give precise dimensions and save precious minutes.

Beware of heat-related deformations... The last weld to close the cube must be done under the constraint of clamps, otherwise not possible.

As decoration in a happy teenager's room
