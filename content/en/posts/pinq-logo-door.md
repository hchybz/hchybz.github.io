Rethinking digital identity through the lens of recycling and circular design, that's the challenge met with this project for the inauguration of this HPC and this room in winter 2024.

🔧 The PINQ logo merges modern design, high-tech upcycling and innovation to embody a strong and committed digital identity: it's the first with 3D components (heat sinks, DIMMs) added to the cut electronic boards.

💡 This creation reaffirms that it is possible to strengthen innovation with an eco-responsible approach.

📸 Scroll 👉 to discover the behind-the-scenes of this custom design, from the first ideas to the visual achievement. Many thanks to Anderson A.!
