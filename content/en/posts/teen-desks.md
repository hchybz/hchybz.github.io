From a single Pinterest image, respecting classic or specific constraints to make these desks comfortable, the challenge is to create legs that are both original and stable.
First welds with really acute angles, no possibility of grinding inside, the cuts must be perfect

Without any special treatment, just with Rutsol type oil. And here are 2 delighted teens
