During the relocation of this HPC from one room to another... the first failed blades were set aside.... once the components were recovered, the blade chassis should have gone to the industrial waste bin... They were ultimately used to create a TV/Amplifier cabinet. This is the first in a series to use this concept of metal chassis with IT components and glass on top.

Possibility to make more.

Dimensions: WDH: 142 x 53 x 41 cm

Weight: ~ 100kg
