Let's change things up a bit... no salvage or recycling this time.

Steel brackets and supports for wisteria and roses to replace the old pressure-treated wood ones.

Powder coating by [Yvelines Sablage](https://www.linkedin.com/in/yvelines-sablage-178339254/), always impeccable.

Some threaded rods were too short following the facade restoration. Had to innovate by extending them or having special nuts machined.

After spending the winter on the ground or sprawled on the roof, the beautiful vegetation was able to return to its new supports.
