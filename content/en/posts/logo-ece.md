Giving visual meaning to a committed collaboration, that's the heart of this project.

🤝 To celebrate the partnership between the High-Tech startup [EXAION](https://www.linkedin.com/company/exaion/) and the engineering school [ECE](https://www.linkedin.com/school/ece-ecole-ingenieurs/), this logo was conceived as the symbol of a technical and sustainable exchange.

🔧 An original creation mixing high-tech recycling and innovative design, illustrating both the technological foundation and the educational dimension of the project.

First creation with black printed circuit boards, from mining motherboards
