## In anticipation of a big sister

Prototype for a possible future 12-person meeting table. This small coffee table showcases what 8 Hitachi FMD (Flash Memory Module) disks look like. How the layout and steel structure should be assembled. 4mm tempered glass.

This one is available for sale.

For nostalgic electronics enthusiasts with graying hair... each purple capacitor is 30 farads, which is absolutely enormous (200 farads total) and even potentially dangerous when the disk is removed from its chassis and the capacitors are not discharged.

* Dimensions (WxDxH): 66 x 67 x 44 cm
* Weight: ~ 20 kg
