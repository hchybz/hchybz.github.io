EXAION Logo - Rack

Herve (ChybzArt)
October 22, 2022
IT
recycling rack datacenter

The first logo on a perforated IT rack door. Validates the method of mounting spacers and the cut A0 template…

At that time, surface components were removed with a Dremel or screwdriver. So no 3D effect like in the next ones. But less risk of damaging the logo when performing rack equipment maintenance.

Still at the entrance of the server room for a guaranteed wow effect!
